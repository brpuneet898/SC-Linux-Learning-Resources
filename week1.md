- `groups`
- 10 letter code - 1st letter type, next 3 - owner, next 3 - group, next 3 - other permissions.
- regular, d directory, l link, c character, b block, s socket, p pipe. 
- `ls -i filename`
- 7 rwx
- 6 rw-
- 5 r-x
- 4 r--
- 2 -w-
- 1 --x
- Read, write and execute
